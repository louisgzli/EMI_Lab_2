About Us

The Evolving Machine Intelligence (EMI) Group focuses on evolvable intelligent systems. We are motivated to understand how evolution generates complexity, diversity and intelligence by computational simulations. A major focus is on neuroevolution, where large-scale deep neural networks are evolved for structure optimization. We are also interested in combining deep learning and evolutionary computation to develop optimization methods.

Reserach Interest

  • Evolutionary Computation  (EC): surrogate-assisted optimization, multi/many-objective optimization, large-scale optimization, and real-world problems.

  • Deep Learning   (DL): image classification and translation with GANs, object detection and segmentation, and multi-domain/multi-modal image translation.

  • EC and DL: architecture optimization using evolutionary algorithm and deep learning based evolutionary algorithm.
